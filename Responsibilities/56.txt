Overview & Responsibilities

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITY: Lead the partner programs compensation space for plans and structures. Leads efforts to improve existing processes or programs and design new plans or processes as required. Inspires confidence within the team, senior management, and the business community communicating improvements and efficiencies.

JOB COMPLEXITY: Evaluating partner plan effectiveness, compensation efficiency, and competitive analyses for the partner program. Collaborating with internal stakeholders as well as coordinating across functions, internally and externally, to ensure delivery on schedule. Developing and documenting business processes. Evaluating business processes and identifying areas where efficiencies may be gained.

SUPERVISION: Operates under minimal supervision. May provide work direction to individual contributors.
