Data Scientist
Key Role:
Help lead the design and development of machine learning algorithms and engaging data visualizations as part of an innovative data science team. Perform highly complex analysis, design, development, testing, and debugging of developed algorithms. Analyze and apply advanced methods, theories, and research techniques to the investigation and testing of complex and advanced software applications and problems. Use technical and analytical expertise to explore and examine data from multiple disparate sources with the goal of discovering patterns and previously hidden insights that can provide a competitive advantage or address a pressing business problem. Apply expertise in CS, software development, and the latest technologies to analyze and implement analysis infrastructure and tools, analytic workflow processes, and complex data visualizations. Leverage comprehension of mathematical and statistical concepts, CS, or domain expertise to bridge the gap among business analysts, engineers, technologists, and mathematicians to ensure software solutions meet analytic requirements.
