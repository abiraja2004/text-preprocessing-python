Information Assurance Analyst
Key Role:
Provide client support in solving all phases of DIACAP and RMF and leverage expertise in the use of eMass. Review and recommend solutions to client problems based on knowledge of products and systems test results. Provide detailed documentation to support C&A or A&A decision by the authorizing official. Conduct and develop POA&M resulting from security assessments. Conduct systems security analysis, design assurance, configuration management, integration, and testing documentation support. This position is located at JBSA-Randolph Air Force Base, TX.
